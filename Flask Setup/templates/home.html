About STEP Gen

STEP Gen is a platform designed to help you generate and customise STEP papers with a whole bunch of different topics and questions from past papers. You can create custom papers, generate random or 'Intelligen' papers, each off which will get you one 'step' closer to that university offer!

How to Use STEP Gen

To get started, head to your Dashboard where you can generate new papers, enter scores, view previous papers, and more. To use our 'Intelligen' feature, make sure to input your scores for completed papers so we can generate papers that are tailored to your needs. This is your personalised hub to manage your learning journey and help you tackle STEP topics that you find difficult.
